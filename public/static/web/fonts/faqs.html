FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions and valuable insights on our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) page. We understand that every entrepreneurial journey is unique, and our FAQs aim to provide clarity on various aspects of the Women Entrepreneurs Division.

Tabs

What is the Women Entrepreneurs Division (WED)?

The Women Entrepreneurs Division is a dedicated division of Trade Development Authority of Pakistan (TDAP) focused on supporting and empowering women women-led businesses across Pakistan. We provide resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities to develop entrepreneurial spirit among women helping them succeed in their business endeavors.

Who is eligible to join the Women Entrepreneurs Division?

The Women Entrepreneurs Division is open to women at all stages of their entrepreneurial journey, from aspiring entrepreneurs to established business owners.

What services and resources does the Women Entrepreneurs Division offer?

We offer a range of services including mentorship programs, workshops, networking events, national & international exhibitions, access to funding opportunities, educational resources, and tailored business support.

How can I register with Women Entrepreneurs Division?

Becoming a member of our community is easy! Simply visit our website and follow the registration process. Membership may be subject to certain criteria, which will be clearly outlined during the registration process.

Is there a cost associated for registration with Women Entrepreneurs Division?

Registration with the Women Entrepreneurs Division is free. You can have access to latest information about various activities and you can get your success story published on our website.

How can I find a mentor through the Women Entrepreneurs Division?

We have a series of mentorship program that matches experienced entrepreneurs with aspiring or newer entrepreneurs. Once you become a member, you'll have access to our mentorship platform where you can connect with potential mentors. You can access our Annual Business Plan to learn about various planned activities.

How I attend events and workshops hosted by the Women Entrepreneurs Division?

You can check on our website regarding the latest planned activities and upcoming events. Also, you can get yourself registered for the trainings through registration from given on home page. If you have suggestions new trainings, please give your feedback on our official email address, or you may reach out to our officers in HQs, or regional offices.

Does the Women Entrepreneurs Division have quota reserved for women-owned businesses?

Yes, at least 10% of the quota is reserved for women-led businesses in TDAP’s events. TDAP supports women entrepreneurs’ participation in international exhibitions and fairs. Participation of WEs’ is permitted in all product categories.

Does the Women Entrepreneurs Division offer subsidies for women-owned businesses?

Yes, we offer 50% subsidy on participation fee on all TDAP flagship events, as well as international events. For international delegations, we offer daily allowances and 50% airfare reimbursement to encourage women participation in trade delegations.

How can I collaborate with the Women Entrepreneurs Division as a volunteer or contributor?

We're always looking for passionate individuals/institutes who want to support our mission. Please reach out on our official email address if you have any ideas on volunteering, contributing, or collaborating with us.

How can I contact the Women Entrepreneurs Division for further assistance?

You can reach out to us through the contact information given in “contact us” tab on our website or by emailing. Additionally, you can find us on social media platforms like Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn for updates and direct messaging.
